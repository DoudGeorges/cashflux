## General Policy

It is the policy of Brim to pay for all reasonable expenses incurred by team members while doing business for Brim. Team members are expected to exercise good judgment with respect to any expenses and check the accuracy of bills before paying or accepting them.

### Key Rules

- All expenses **over $50.00** must be **pre-authorized** by your manager.
- **Receipts are required** before any expense is reimbursed.
- Once approved, actual costs directly related to business travel objectives will be reimbursed.
- Receipts should be submitted within the **current month** (best effort).
- **Falsifying expense reports** to reflect costs not incurred is **expressly prohibited**.

---

## Business Travel

### Supplier Entertainment

- Reasonable entertainment of customers is acceptable.
- **Names of guests and purpose** must be listed with receipts.
- Expensing **alcoholic beverages is not permitted** unless dining with a customer.

### Tips & Gratuities

- Tips may be expensed up to **15%** for services and porterage.
- Meal tips are included with meal claims and will **not be reimbursed above 20%**.

### Parking

- Reasonable parking expenses may be reimbursed.

### Transportation

- Use the **most efficient and cost-effective** form of transportation.
- **Tolls** will be reimbursed.
- Business travel using **team member–owned vehicles** is permitted.
- Kilometres driven for work are reimbursed at **Canada Revenue Agency rates** (subject to annual change).
- Accidents while traveling on business must be **promptly reported** to your Manager.
- Brim **does not pay** for traffic tickets, parking tickets, or cars rented for personal use.

### Car Rental

- The company will reimburse car rental costs when a rental car is deemed necessary.
- Multiple team members at the same location may be required to **share a car**.
- Non-standard vehicles (4+ company travelers) will be reimbursed accordingly.
- Company or personal credit card must be used for rental.
- **Car rental, parking, and gasoline receipts** are required for reimbursement.

### Car Insurance

- Team members should inform their automobile insurer if using their vehicle for work purposes (may increase premiums).
- Brim **assumes no liability** for loss or accident relating to personal vehicle operation.
- Rented or leased vehicles are treated as personal vehicles under this policy.

---

## Corporate Credit Cards

- Brim has issued corporate credit cards to **select team members** for business expenses.
- Only the **individual named on the card** may use it.
- Team members are **not obligated** to obtain a corporate credit card.
- Team members are **personally responsible** for all business expenses and will be reimbursed through the standard procedure.
- **Personal expenses on corporate cards are prohibited.**
- Brim may **restrict usage or revoke** cards where consistent policy abuse is evident.
- Brim pays the fee for the individual corporate credit card; fees for other personal cards are **not reimbursed**.
- A **lost or stolen card** must be reported immediately to the card issuer.

---

## Company Events

- Team members must exercise common sense and good judgment during all company events.
- While a festive atmosphere is encouraged, these are **business events** — all company policies regarding team member behavior remain applicable.
- Brim supports a **"Don't Drink and Drive"** policy and forbids any team member from driving while impaired following any work function.
- Team members who consume alcohol at company events should **pre-arrange transportation home**.
- If necessary, the company will arrange and/or pay for transportation.

---

## Company Property

- Preserving and safeguarding Brim's property is **each team member's responsibility**.
- Equipment, materials, and supplies are Brim's property and must be used **only for company business**.
- All such assets must be protected from **theft, misuse, or damage**.
- Team members may be **disciplined** for loss or damage due to negligence.
- No company property may be borrowed for personal use **without management authorization**.
- Theft of company or co-worker property may result in **criminal charges**.
- All company-owned assets (laptops, smartphones, electronic passes, etc.) must be **returned upon termination or resignation**.
